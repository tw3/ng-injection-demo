"Why isn't my data shared?"

The bug: A developer creates two sibling widgets that both provide TrackerService at the component level. They expect incrementing the counter in Widget A to also update Widget B. But it doesn't — each widget has its own instance!

The fix: Remove providers from both widgets and provide the service at a shared ancestor (parent component, route, or root). This way, both widgets inherit the same singleton instance.
